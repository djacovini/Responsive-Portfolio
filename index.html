About David

David is a battle-tested entrepreneur with strong background in finance and business development.

Most recently David had served as the Founder and President of Innovator Management LLC and the President, Treasurer, and a Trustee of the Academy Funds Trust. There he built a boutique investment product provider designing and launching leading-edge mutual funds and exchange-traded funds. In 2017 the Innovator business was sold to Innovator Capital Management, LLC.

David’s varied finance career is a testament to his analytical ability and prudent judgment. His Wall Street career included trading interest rate products, running a strategy group, and structuring and marketing derivatives ranging from vanilla products to complex exotic structures.

David’s financial practice is rooted in the theoretical understanding he developed in his academic studies. He earned his Master of Business Administration from the MIT Sloan School of Management with a concentration in Financial Engineering in 2002 and his Bachelor of Arts from the College of the Holy Cross in 1997.
